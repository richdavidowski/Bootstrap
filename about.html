About Floppy Dog WoodShop

Floppy Dog WoodShop is a passion project of its founder Richie Davidowski who has had a life-long passion for wood-working. The "Floppy Dog" name is of course an homage to Richie's beloved basset hound sidekick, Rambo. Started in a small shed in the backyard of his rental-home in Fort Collins, Richie originally set out to build custom humidor cigar boxes, but quickly expanded his designs to include coffee tables, book shelves, as well as other custom furniture pieces.
